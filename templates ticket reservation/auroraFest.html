Mark your calendars for an electrifying weekend because the Philippines' Largest Music Festival is back in Clark on April 6-7, 2024!

This year, EP!C Events is pulling out all the stops, bringing you the BIGGEST names in the Philippine Music Industry. But that's not all - brace yourselves for an even more spectacular sight as larger-than-life hot air balloons illuminate the Clark skyline, creating
an unforgettable backdrop for a weekend filled with music and magic.
